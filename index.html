Independent Advice Explained

Why Most ‘Independent’ Advice Is Anything But...

Real estate agents, including "property research houses", hold listings so they are legally obligated to work for the first person who pays them (ie. the vendor, not you).

A horrible secret, many agents source ‘quotas’ of houses from developers to attract higher commissions, then they reverse engineer the research to make it look like you're getting a good deal.

As a result, your ‘independent’ advice has been tainted by this conflict of interest, and the person who loses is you!

At Visionworks we only represent your interests. The small sign-up fee ensures a legal, as well as a personal obligation to put your best interests first.

Our clients stay with us for many investments, so as your portofolio grows, we grow too.

Reduce Your Risk - We’ve Thought Ahead

Less Stress. Higher Returns

Buying the wrong property can cause mountains of stress and set you back years. Our buyer advocacy model was designed specifically to reduce this risk.

Visionworks property recommendations come with hundreds of hours of research and decades of experience.

Why Kahla Gerard?

Having Kahla in your corner gives you access to a lifetime of financial and property investment experience to help you avoid common buyer mistakes.

Kahla literally grew up in real estate. Her father trained as a lawyer in France and when he migrated to Australia, worked as a real estate agent, before becoming a successful property developer. The 90's "recession we had to have" forced him into bankruptcy.

Witnessing first-hand how painful this was, and having lived through five recessions herself, Kahla has made it her mission to save investors from costly mistakes.

Working first as a financial advisor, then a mortgage broker, Kahla saw how much misleading advice is peddled inside the property industry. This motivated her to establish VisionWorks so she could guide investors clear of the many potential pitfalls that come with buying property.

Her concern and commitment to assist clients make the best decisions can sometimes keep her awake at night. This level of care is how you know you’re getting advice truly focused on your goals.
